## 2. Website / Storefront Plan

### 2.1. Platform Choice

Pick **one**:

- **Shopify** (recommended if you want easiest e-commerce tools):
  - Pros: Smooth checkout, built-in payments, lots of themes, supports growth.
  - Cons: Monthly cost (~&#36;39), but might be worth it if you want scalability.

- **Etsy** (quick & cheap to start):
  - Pros: Built-in audience searching for ‚ÄúPokemon cup‚Äù, easier start.
  - Cons: Less brand control; fees on each sale; can‚Äôt fully customize checkout.

- **Simple landing page + Stripe Checkout**:
  - Pros: Very low-cost, simple.
  - Cons: More manual setup, fewer built-in ecommerce features.

**Practical suggestion:** 
If you want fast traction with low tech headache, **start with Etsy + a simple one-page site** that links to the Etsy listing. Later, move to Shopify when you get consistent sales.
